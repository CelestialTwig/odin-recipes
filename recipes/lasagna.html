How to make lasagna

Prepare ingredients: Cook lasagna noodles, brown ground beef (or other protein), and make a simple tomato sauce with garlic, onions, and seasonings. You’ll also need ricotta cheese, mozzarella, and Parmesan cheese.

Layering: Start with a thin layer of sauce in a baking dish. Then add a layer of noodles, followed by ricotta, mozzarella, and sauce. Repeat the layers (noodles, cheese, sauce) until the dish is full, finishing with a layer of sauce and a generous amount of mozzarella on top.

Bake: Cover the lasagna with foil and bake in a preheated oven at 375°F (190°C) for about 40 minutes. Remove the foil for the last 10 minutes to brown the top.

Cool and serve: Let it cool for a few minutes before slicing and serving. Enjoy!

Ingredients

  • Ground beef (or a mix of beef and pork)
  • Onion (chopped)
  • Garlic (minced)
  • Canned crushed tomatoes or tomato sauce
  • Tomato paste
  • Italian seasoning (basil, oregano, thyme)
  • Salt and pepper
  • Red wine (optional, for deeper flavor)
  • Olive oil
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Mozzarella cheese (shredded)
  • Parmesan cheese (grated)
  • Egg (helps bind the mixture)
  • Fresh parsley or basil (optional, for added flavor)
  • Salt and pepper
  • Additional mozzarella cheese (for topping)
  • Lasagna noodles (either regular or no-boil)
